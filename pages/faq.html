FAQ

Q. What does 4Shops sell?
Professional bodyshop and detailing supplies — 3M tapes and primers, Meguiar's cleaners and care products, SprayMax coatings, and related shop essentials for collision repair, refinish, and daily vehicle care.

Q. Are the products authentic?
Yes. We source from authorized distributors and established manufacturers. Counterfeit or grey-market products are not covered by manufacturer warranties — buy from 4Shops or an authorized retailer to ensure authenticity.

Q. Which brands do you carry?
Our catalog focuses on pro-grade names bodyshops already use: 3M, Meguiar's, SprayMax, High Teck, SEM, USC, U-POL, POR-15, and others. Browse by category on the site or visit our full catalog.

Q. How do I choose the right tape or primer?
Product pages include manufacturer descriptions and application notes. For adhesion work, match the tape type to the substrate (foil for HVAC, VHB for permanent bonding, molding tape for trim). Use 3M Primer 94 on low-surface-energy plastics and vinyl before applying tape or wrap. If you are unsure, email contact@4shops.us with your application and we will point you to the right SKU.

Q. Do you sell wholesale or to shops?
Yes. For volume orders or recurring shop supply needs, contact contact@4shops.us with your business details.

Q. How long does it take for my order to ship?
Orders typically ship within 24–48 hours on business days.

Q. How much does shipping cost?
United States & Canada
Free standard shipping (3–7 business days) and free returns on orders over $200. Express shipping available regionally.

Q. Which countries do you ship to?
We ship throughout North America and to select international markets. See checkout for availability in your region, or contact us if your country is not listed.

Q. Can I track my order?
When your order ships, you receive a confirmation email with the carrier and tracking link.

Q. What if I am not home for delivery?
The carrier may leave the package in a safe location or attempt redelivery. Follow the instructions on the delivery notice they leave.

Q. How should I store tapes and chemicals?
Keep products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Seal containers after use. Tapes perform best when stored flat at moderate temperature — extreme heat or cold can affect adhesive before application.

Q. How do I get the best bond from 3M tape?
Surfaces must be clean, dry, and free of wax, silicone, and loose contamination. Wipe with appropriate cleaner, apply primer where recommended (especially on plastics and vinyl), then press firmly along the full width. Allow adequate dwell time before stress or heat cycling.

Q. Are Meguiar's products safe on all surfaces?
Each product is formulated for specific materials — leather, plastic trim, paint, etc. Read the label and test in an inconspicuous area first. Avoid using interior leather conditioners on exterior rubber or unpainted trim unless the product specifies it.

Q. What is the shelf life of opened products?
Shelf life varies by product and manufacturer. Use within the period noted on the packaging, and replace if consistency, colour, or performance changes.

How do 4Shops Gift Cards work?
Gift cards are delivered by email and include a unique redemption code. They can be used toward any product in our shop (except another gift card). Unused balance stays on the code for future purchases.

Gift cards cannot be refunded or exchanged.

Do gift cards expire?
No.

Can I send a gift card to someone else?
The code is sent to the purchaser — forward or print the email to give it as a gift.

How do I check my balance?
Email contact@4shops.us with your gift card number.

Q. Are duties and taxes included in the price?
For eligible destinations, our Delivered Duty Paid (DDP) service includes import duties and taxes in the checkout total — no surprise fees on delivery.

Availability of DDP depends on your shipping country. If DDP is not offered at checkout, local import charges may apply on delivery.

Q. Which payment methods do you accept?
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay, Discover, Diners Club, Bancontact, iDEAL, and Interac.

Q. Does the listed price include taxes?
Canada & United States: Sales tax is calculated at checkout. Canadian domestic orders have no additional import fees.

Q. How do returns and exchanges work?
Unused items in original packaging may be returned within the window stated in our refund policy. Opened chemicals, aerosols, and cut tape rolls may not be eligible — contact contact@4shops.us before returning.

Q. What if my order arrives damaged?
Email contact@4shops.us with your order number and photos of the damage. We will arrange a replacement or refund.
